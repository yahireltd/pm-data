## Problem
Capture the human playbook — what good salespeople *do and say* at each level/process — so it's repeatable and
teachable, not tribal knowledge.

## Design notes
Per level/process: the actions + the talk track. System = automated (none); Incubation = qualify + light nurture;
Account = protect + grow the relationship; Strategic = consultative, bespoke, cross-department. Source for scripts
(T-0490) and activities (T-0491); rolls up into the per-cohort playbooks (T-0496).
