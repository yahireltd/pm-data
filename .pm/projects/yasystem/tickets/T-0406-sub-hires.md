Yo both,

There’s been quite a few more sub hire related issues last week, including people loading sub hired orders with normal stock and not loading the return.

It is disappointing because the info is there on the contracts it’s a subhire, but sub hires are confusing to people as they a delivery is a collection and vise versa.

If we are continuing with Sub-hires at this level, I think it does warrant a bit of system development to make the process clearer for the warehouse team. As much as I’d like to say, we can be on top of it with internal communications, it’s just not proving to work.

What I think is needed (much like how insphire did):

* First date (pick-up) shows as a collection
* Return date shows a delivery
* The order looks visually different, e.g. different colour and not a C0 number
* Automated stock adjustment accounting for the sub-hire (assuming hiring own stock)

Let me know thoughts.
