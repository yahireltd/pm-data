**-How often does this happen? **

The request often comes up from the customers side, usually when a cancelled / postponed order, however, from the sales side this does not come up as often.

**-What do we offer?** Flexible across different event sizes 

* Time-limited credit to be used against a future booking
* Free setup & breakdown
* Free or discounted early or late delivery/ collection

**-Why don’t we just do a discount/credit note?**

According to the sales ( this is from different conversation I have had, it’s not an official statement) this will be reflected on their targets.

**-How do we log it now?** 

* We can come up with a voucher code that includes the name of the person that issued it, expiry/ issue date. i.e. Sam2522026
* We could log them on the system ( customer profile) or excel or a new page ( being cheeky here)
* Both ways should have like a status ( available/ redeemed/ expired)
