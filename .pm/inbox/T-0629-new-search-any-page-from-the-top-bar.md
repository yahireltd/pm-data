Hi all,

  

We've added a quick page search to YaSystem so you can jump straight to a page instead of hunting through the menus.

Where: the search box with the 🔍 icon at the top of the page (placeholder "Search pages…").

  

How: start typing a page name (a couple of letters is enough), e.g. ledger, quotes, stock, matching pages appear as you type; click one (or press Enter) to go straight there.

How the results are ordered:

- Pages that are already in your menu come first, marked with a ☰ menu icon and highlighted, so your everyday pages are right at the top.

- Any other pages you have access to appear below, alphabetically.

  

A couple of things to know:

- It only shows pages you have access to, results are tailored to each person's permissions.

- It's desktop only for now, it won't appear on phones or tablets.

  

Give it a go, it's the quickest way to get around.

Any problems, email support@yahire.com.
