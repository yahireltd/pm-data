  Fleet Check replacement program:\


  Legal requirements

   

  HGV drivers **must carry out a daily walkaround inspection** *before they drive the vehicle* — this is a legal duty to help ensure roadworthiness and safety. It applies to all commercial HGVs.

  Each driver must do their own check **before using the vehicle that day**, even if another driver has already done a check earlier.

  If faults are found, they **must be reported and recorded**, and unsafe vehicles **must not be driven** until defects are fixed.

  For light vans, there is **not a specific legal requirement** that every driver must record daily checks in the same way as HGVs. However:

  It is **strongly recommended** to carry out daily checks (lights, tyres, brakes, etc.) to ensure the vehicle is **safe** and avoids offences for driving a dangerous vehicle.
