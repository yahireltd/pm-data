# **Strategic Goals**

**1. Acquire More Customers**

* Generate more opportunities
* Win more venues, caterers, agencies, exhibitions, corporates and major events
* Expand market share

**2. Maximise Customer Value**

* Increase conversion
* Increase repeat business
* Increase share of wallet
* Develop strategic accounts

**3. Deliver a Better Customer Experience**

* Faster responses
* Better communication
* Reduced friction
* Consistent service

**4. Build Competitive Advantage**

* Stronger customer relationships
* Strategic integrations
* Better data
* Better systems
* Customer "moats"

**5. Create a Learning Organisation**

* Measure performance
* Coach people
* Improve continuously
* Learn from data
* Build feedback loops

***

**Core Capabilities Required**

**Customer Segmentation**

Know who customers are and what type of process they require.

**Opportunity Prioritisation**

Identify:

* Golden nuggets
* High value opportunities
* Strategic opportunities
* Quick wins

**Sales Process Management**

Define:

* Quick conversion process
* In-depth conversion process
* Lifetime account process

**Strategic Account Management**

Support:

* Account plans
* Organograms
* Relationship mapping
* Share-of-wallet growth

**Performance Management**

Provide:

* KPIs
* Dashboards
* Lead indicators
* Lag indicators
* Reviews

**Continuous Improvement**

Enable:

* Feedback loops
* Machine learning
* Testing
* Process refinement

***

**Project Phases**

**Phase 1: Sales Efficiency**

**Goal:** Remove waste and improve productivity.

Examples:

* Menu tidy-up
* Duplicate page removal
* Speed improvements
* Process finishing
* Email templates
* AI email assistance
* UX improvements

**Outcome:** Same people can process more work with less effort.

***

**Phase 2: Sales Operating System Foundation**

**Goal:** Create a structured and scalable sales engine.

Examples:

* Customer segmentation
* Customer scoring
* Golden nugget identification
* Workflow definitions
* Process design
* Task frameworks

**Outcome:** Right customer receives the right level of attention.

***

**Phase 3: Management & Learning System**

**Goal:** Improve performance through visibility and accountability.

Examples:

* Dashboards
* KPIs
* Activity tracking
* Manager reviews
* Conformance reviews
* Predictive indicators

**Outcome:** Managers can systematically improve results.

***

**Phase 4: Strategic Account Platform**

**Goal:** Grow major accounts and increase share of wallet.

Examples:

* Account plans
* Organograms
* Relationship mapping
* Opportunity plans
* Share-of-wallet tracking
* Strategic reviews

**Outcome:** Yahire systematically grows its most valuable customers.

***

**Guiding Principle**

**Automate and systemise wherever possible.**

Humans should spend less time:

* Administering
* Chasing
* Sorting
* Reacting

And more time:

* Building relationships
* Solving problems
* Developing accounts
* Closing opportunities

The system should progressively move work up the value ladder while continuously learning and improving.
